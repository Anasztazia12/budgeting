Help & Guide

A quick overview of every feature in the app.

🚀 Getting started

There are two ways to use the app:

  • Registered user – create a free account with a username, email and password. Once registered, sign in using your username or email address and your password. Your data is saved to the cloud and is available on any device.
  • Guest mode – no account needed. Data is saved only for the current browser tab and is permanently deleted when you close it or sign out.

Password rules: 6–20 characters, must include at least one lowercase letter, one uppercase letter, and one number.

📋 Budget page

The main page for recording your expected income and expenses.

Income form

  • Category – choose the type of income (Salary, Child Benefit, Universal Credit, etc.).
  • Amount – enter the value (decimals allowed).
  • Date – when do you expect to receive this income?
  • Note – optional short description (max 280 characters).
  • Show in every month – tick this to make the entry repeat every month automatically.

Expense form

Same fields as income. Categories include Rent, Mortgage, Car, Groceries, Electricity, and many more.

Statistics cards

  • Total income / Expected income – sum of income entries in the selected period.
  • Total expenses / Expected expense – sum of expense entries in the selected period.

List filters

Below the forms, use the -tól / -ig date filters to narrow down which entries appear in each list.

Period selector

At the top, set a from and to date to define which period the totals and lists cover.

Today's notice

If any income or expense is due today, a modal pops up when you log in. Tick "Don't show again today" to suppress it for the rest of the day.

🔮 Budget Forecast

Plan ahead and test "what if" scenarios without affecting your real budget.

  • Period selector – choose the date range the forecast should cover.
  • + Add extra / unexpected item – add a one-off income or expense row to the simulation.
  • Each row needs: type (income or expense), amount, date, and an optional note and plan name.
  • Save row (💾) – saves the row as a named scenario so you can reload it later.
  • Add to budget (budget icon) – copies this item directly into your real budget in one click.
  • Close row (✕ black) – removes the row from the view without deleting the saved scenario.
  • Delete plan (✕ red) – permanently deletes the saved scenario (asks for confirmation first).
  • Saved plans – click the dropdown to see your saved scenarios, select one and press Load to restore its rows.

Forecast results

  • Balance up to date – income minus expenses from your real budget within the period.
  • Balance with planned items – the above figure adjusted for all what-if rows.
  • Difference – how much the what-if items change your balance.
  • New balance – your estimated end-of-period balance if all planned items occur.

📊 Summary

A read-only overview of your income and expenses for any selected period.

  • Period selector – choose from and to dates. The cards and lists update automatically.
  • Total income / Total expense – sums for the period.
  • Balance – income minus expenses.
  • Spending to date – expenses up to today's date within the period.
  • Chart – shows all income and expense categories together on a pie or bar chart. Switch between the two using the Type selector.
  • End-of-period projection – an estimate of your closing balance at the end of the selected period.

💳 Debt Manager

Track your loans, repayments and projected payoff dates.

  • Debt name – a label for the loan (e.g. "Car loan").
  • Monthly payment – how much you pay each month.
  • Payment date – the date your next payment is due.
  • Remaining amount – how much you still owe.
  • Due date – optional: when the loan is scheduled to end.
  • Planned early payment date – optional: if you plan to pay it off early, enter the date here.
  • Planned amount – the lump sum you plan to use for early repayment.
  • Add to budget forecast – tick this to automatically create a forecast scenario for this debt's monthly payment.

Statistics cards

  • Total debt – sum of all monthly payments.
  • Remaining – total remaining balances across all debts.
  • Paid – difference between total and remaining.
  • Monthly payments – total amount due each month.

⚙️ Settings & navigation

  • Language – switch between Hungarian (HU) and English (EN) using the selector in the top-left corner. The choice is remembered.
  • Light / Dark theme – toggle in the toolbar. The choice is remembered.
  • Currency – choose HUF, GBP, USD or EUR. Amounts are displayed in the chosen currency (no conversion is applied).
  • Hamburger menu (☰) – access Home, Adatok módosítása (profile), navigation links, Install app, Sign out, and Delete account.
  • Change username – available in the hamburger menu when signed in. You must enter your current username, then the new one twice.
  • Change password – available in the hamburger menu when signed in. Enter your current password, then the new one twice.
  • Auto sign-out – after 5 minutes of inactivity you will be signed out automatically.
  • Install app – on supported browsers you can install the app to your home screen for offline use.

🔄 Recurring entries

Tick "Show in every month" when adding an income or expense to make it appear automatically in every month's view.

  • Recurring entries use the day of the month from their original date.
  • You can exclude individual months by using the toggle on each list item – this removes it from just that month without deleting the entry.
  • When deleting a recurring entry you can choose to delete it from this month only or from all months.
